Welcome to my Tennis Game

Feeling bored at work, lets play Tennis while we release some stress The Tennis Game is a simple 2D sports game where two players compete against each other in a virtual tennis match. Each player controls a paddle, which they can move vertically to hit a ball back and forth over a net. The objective of the game is to prevent the ball from hitting the player's side of the court while trying to score points by making the ball pass the opponent's paddle.
